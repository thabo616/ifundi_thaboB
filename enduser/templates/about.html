About Us

Welcome to AppliancesRus, your number one source for all electronic devices. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2001 by Joe Baloyi, AppliancesRus e-store has come a long way from its beginnings in Johannesburg. When Joe Baloyi first started out, his passion for helping neighbours be more eco-friendly by providing the best appliances at its lowest price which drove him to do intense research, quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over South Africa, & southern africa, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the appliance industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
